# Engineering Mathematics Syllabus

---

## Unit 1: Linear Algebra
* **Matrices and Determinants:** Properties, inverse, and rank of a matrix.
* **Systems of Linear Equations:** Consistency, Crammer’s rule, and Gaussian elimination.
* **Eigenvalues and Eigenvectors:** Characteristic equations, properties of eigenvalues, Cayley-Hamilton Theorem, and diagonalization of matrices.

---

## Unit 2: Discrete Mathematics (Crucial for IT/CSE)
* **Mathematical Logic:** Propositional logic, first-order logic, tautologies, and Boolean algebra.
* **Set Theory & Algebra:** Sets, relations, functions, partial orders, lattices, and groups.
* **Combinatorics:** Permutations, combinations, Pigeonhole principle, recurrence relations, and generating functions.
* **Graph Theory:** Connectivity, matching, coloring, trees, Eulerian and Hamiltonian graphs, and planar graphs.

---

## Unit 3: Probability and Statistics
* **Basic Probability:** Axioms, conditional probability, and Bayes' Theorem.
* **Random Variables:** Discrete and continuous random variables, expectation, variance, and standard deviation.
* **Probability Distributions:** Binomial, Poisson, Normal, Uniform, and Exponential distributions.
* **Statistics:** Correlation, linear regression, and basic hypothesis testing (t-test, z-test).

---

## Unit 4: Calculus
* **Differential Calculus:** Limits, continuity, and differentiability. Mean value theorems, Taylor's and Maclaurin's series.
* **Applications of Derivatives:** Maxima and minima, tangents, and normals.
* **Integral Calculus:** Evaluation of definite and improper integrals. Applications in finding areas and volumes.
* **Partial Derivatives:** Chain rule and directional derivatives.

---

## Unit 5: Numerical Methods (Common in TNEB General Engineering)
* **Root Finding:** Newton-Raphson method, Bisection method, and Secant method.
* **Numerical Integration:** Trapezoidal rule and Simpson’s 1/3 and 3/8 rules.
* **Differential Equations:** Euler’s method and Runge-Kutta (4th order) method.
